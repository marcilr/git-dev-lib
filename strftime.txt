strftime - format date and time


A great looking library with darn near everything.

I particularly like %j for the day of the year a decimal 
number (range 001 to 366).

SYNOPSIS         
  #include <time.h>

  size_t strftime(char *s, size_t max, const char *format,
                  const struct tm *tm);
